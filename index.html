ABOUT ME

My name is Jan Zavrel and I am Biohacker, iOS & Web Developer, Author, Teacher, Lifelong Learner, Evernote Certified Consultant.

My ultimate goal is to live long enough to live forever. I got inspired by Ray Kurzweil's book Transcend: Nine Steps to Living Well Forever. To be alive has a special meaning and value for me. I consider it a privilege to be able to wake up in the morning, inhale a fresh air and greet a brand new day. Unfortunately not everyone has been that lucky. Among them my mother, who died when she was 47, my brother when he was 30, and my wife when she was 26 years young.

My consulting services are focused on nothing less than redesigning your lifestyle so you can live a longer, healthier and happier life. Ultimately, to live forever. I know it sounds like a crazy joke or sci-fi, but it's immensely motivating.

On my journey, I embrace the philosophy of Jeff Olson's Slight Edge. It is constantly reminding me, that I can achieve anything I want. All it takes is to give it just a few minutes each day and stay persistent.
