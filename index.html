About Me

I started my IT career as a Pascal programming language student in 1994. Later I discovered the potential of the web and founded with my colleague WEBXP company which offered websites for businesses.
In 2000 I started working for German-based company at that time, where I was the part of team creating the top of the line webshop for than famous Breuninger Mode & Beauty online kaufen.
I also started my very own project and designed, developed and published one of the first so called discussion servers, that were very popular in my country years before the Facebook became worldwide success.
In 2008 when iPhone SDK was introduced I moved to mobile apps world and soon published my first verb dictionary for iPhone.
In 2015 I founded BOHEMIAPPS which evolved from simple iOS applications platform to technology education project.
I love to learn new things and I love to share more knowledge with others even more. I believe the student is only as successful in understanding the topic as is his teacher in explaining it. In other words - there's no bad student, only the bad instructor.
My mission is to share what I know and present it in best possible way I can provide so my students can benefit from it. I respect their time and hope to bring them only the best quality education.
